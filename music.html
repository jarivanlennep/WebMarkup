Live Music

Want to get groovy and listen to live music in a cozy Amsterdam café? Discover Amsterdam's live music scene with our top picks:

Maloe Melo

Malou Mello is the oldest blues and rock cafe in Amsterdam. It's a small intimate place where the locals gn. A lot of famous likues and rock musicians have played here. They have live music seven days of the week every night from 9 Pry until 3 AM. if you want to see where the locals go for live blues and rock, check it out!

The full agenda is on their main website:https://www.maloemeko.com

Alto

This is another intimate live music spot for the jarz-lovers. It has a cozy and artistic vibe from the inside with lots of photos of famous musicians. It's also close to Museumplein, so after checking out Van Gogh Museum or Rijksmuseum you can give it a visit. If you're looking for some serious live jazz performances Alto is a must.

Further information and opening hours on their website: https://www.jazz-cafe-alho.nl

The Melkweg

If you're not looking for a small cozy place with jazz or blues, and you just want to go out, the Melkweg is the place to go. Translating to "Milky Way' in Dutch, the Melkweg used to be a milk factory. Now the large interior space is used as a venue for music every night. Whether you're a hip hop fan, an indie fan, or a pop fan, Melkweg has you covered. Every night they have a different genre, for which you can get ticketa. A lot of famous musicians of all genres have played here and play here often.

If you want to go, most of the time you will need tickets, so if you're interested you can check out their schedule and their tickets here:https://www.melkweg.nl
