Museums

Want to see the best art of the Netherlands and indulge in its rich culture and history? Check out our favourite picks in Amsterdam:

Anne Frank House

Learn about Anne Frank's story and discover the secret place where she hid during World War 2. If you want to learn about the history of Amsterdam, this is a must-see. The Anne Frank House is so popular that there are often long lines (as seen in the picture) and you have to book your visit at least three days before.

Get more information and book here:https://www.annefrank.org/en/

Van Gogh Museum

See Van Gogh's amazing paintings in this museum all about Van Gogh. You can also learn about his life and his creative process. This museum got 1400 original Van Gogh paintings including his famous Sunflowers painting. When you're done there is a separate building with a souvenir shop where you can buy beautiful Van Gogh style souvenirs. The museum is a must-visit for art enthusiasts.

If you want more information or if you want to buy tickets visit their website here:https://www.vangoghmuseum.nl/en

Rijksmuseum

The Rijksmuseum is on the same Museum Square as the Van Gogh Museum and has masterpieces of all the best painters in Dutch history. You're going to see the famous "Night Watch" from Rembrandt and the "Milk Maid" from Vermeer. We recommend the guided tour to learn a lot about Dutch culture and history and its artistic evolution. Whether you're an art fanatic or if you like history, this museum is a cultural treasure you shouldn't miss.
